Famous Places in India

  • Taj Mahal
  • Qutub Minar
  • Jaipur
  • Varanasi

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, an iconic symbol of India, is a white marble mausoleum located in Agra. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, it is known for its stunning architecture and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Qutub Minar

The Qutub Minar is a towering minaret in Delhi, built in 1193 by Qutb-ud-din Aibak. It stands at 73 meters tall and is the tallest brick minaret in the world. It is also a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Jaipur

Jaipur, known as the Pink City, is famous for its historic palaces and forts, such as the Hawa Mahal and Amber Fort. It is the capital of Rajasthan and a major tourist destination in India.

Varanasi

Varanasi, one of the oldest cities in the world, is located on the banks of the Ganges River. It is a major religious and cultural center, known for its ghats and temples.
